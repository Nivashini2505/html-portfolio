My favorite movies of all time

Top 3 movies that comes to my mind now

Unakum enakum

The film stars Jayam Ravi as Santhosh, a rich NRI who falls in love with Kavitha, a village girl played by Trisha. The film follows their love story and the challenges they face from their families and society.

M.Kumaran son of Mahalakshmi

The film stars Jayam Ravi as Kumaran, a kickboxer who shares a close bond with his mother Mahalakshmi, played by Nadhiya.

Santhosh Subramaniam

Santhosh Subramaniam is a romantic comedy film directed by M. Raja and starring Jayam Ravi and Genelia. The film revolves around the relationship between a father who is overprotective of his son and a son who wants to make his own choices in life
